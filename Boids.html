Controls

1: Decrease cohesion radius
2: Increase cohesion radius
3: Decrease cohesion priority
4: Increase cohesion priority
A: Decrease alignment radius
D: Increase alignment priority
S: Decrease alignment priority
W: Increase alignment priority
Left Arrow: Decrease separation radius
Right Arrow: Increase separation radius
Down Arrow: Decrease separation priority
Up Arrow: Increase separation priority
O: Turns Repulsive Force On/Off
T: Changes behaviour



Wandering

This behaviour is the primary behaviour for the boids. It allows the boids to travel however they like which is literally just wandering around.


Cohesion



This behaviour allows the boids to come together like a magnet.


Separation



This behaviours makes the boids try and move apart from each other


Repulsive Force



This behaviours is similar to the Separation behaviour, but it only applies to one boids and it makes the other boids run away.
