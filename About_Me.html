About ME




Upon completing my studies in May 2024 at Champlain College in Burlington Vermont I will have received my bachelor’s degree in computer science majoring in
“Game Programming”. While attending Champlain College I have acquired a strong foundation in programming languages such as Unity, Unreal Engine, C++, C#,
and HTML. Having the knowledge and now experience using these various languages I’ve had the opportunity to put my programming skills to work as I’ve
completed several projects in a team environment. Our final project entitled “Molementum” was a game we successfully created with a team consisting of me, a
second programmer, an illustrator, project manager and an animator. The excitement of being part of the game creation process is the reason I chose this career
path.


Some personal things about me, I’m from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, my hobbies include watching Anime and in my free time taking on challenging brain activities
such as puzzle solving. I enjoy spending time with my family and traveling.
